# Risk Policy

## First 30 days

- No live trading.
- No leverage.
- No margin.
- No options.
- No crypto.
- No short selling.
- No strategy may risk more than a small predefined demo allocation.
- No trading based only on AI-generated reasoning.
- Every signal must be logged.

## Strategy risk template

Each strategy must define:

```text
Strategy name:
Hypothesis:
Market condition:
Entry:
Exit:
Stop:
Position size:
Max daily loss:
Max open positions:
Known failure mode:
Why this may be wrong:
```

## Human review

AI can suggest, code, summarize, and critique.

AI must not be the final authority for financial decisions.
